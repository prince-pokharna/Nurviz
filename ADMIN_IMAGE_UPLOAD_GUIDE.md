## ğŸ”§ Troubleshooting

1. **File Size**

   - Compress it: https://tinypng.com

   - Is connection stable?
   - Try uploading 1 image first

4. **Browser**
   - Try Chrome (recommended)
   - Clear cache or use incognito mode

5. **Login**
   - Are you logged in as admin?
   - Try logging out and back in
